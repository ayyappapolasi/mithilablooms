Who We Are

Rooted in Nature,
Driven by Purpose

Mithila Blooms Pvt Ltd. is a medium-scale organic agri and exports company with over 10 years of experience in sustainable cultivation and international trade. We specialize in the farming of Dutch roses and the prized Arka Savi rose variety, alongside the export of premium organic coffee beans and cashew nuts.

Every seed we sow and every harvest we ship carries our unwavering commitment to chemical-free, ecologically responsible farming. Our operations are fully certified organic — from soil preparation through to post-harvest processing and export packaging.

Our Promise

Why Choose Organic?

We believe that what's good for the earth is good for you.

Zero Pesticides

All our produce is grown entirely without synthetic chemicals. Natural pest management and composted organic inputs are the only inputs we use.

Soil & Ecosystem Health

Healthy soil grows healthy crops. Our regenerative practices maintain biodiversity, prevent erosion and enrich the land for future generations.

Third-Party Certified

Our organic status is verified by internationally recognised certification bodies. Every batch ships with full traceability documentation.

Water Conservation

Drip irrigation and rainwater harvesting ensure minimal water waste while maintaining optimal crop hydration across all our farms.

Better for Health

Organic products retain higher levels of antioxidants, vitamins and minerals. No chemical residues means cleaner food on your table.

Community First

We partner with local farming communities, ensuring fair wages, safe working conditions and knowledge-sharing in organic agricultural practices.
