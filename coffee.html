Why Araku Coffee

Our Araku coffee is sourced from the highlands of the Araku Valley in Andhra Pradesh, a region internationally recognized for producing premium-quality, organic, and specialty coffee. Grown by tribal and smallholder farmers under shade and regenerative farming systems, Araku coffee is valued for its clean cup profile, balanced flavor, and traceable origin.

  • 100% Organic Certification
  • Handpicked Cherries for superior quality
  • Shade-Grown under regenerative farming
  • Direct Farm-to-Cup Model

What Makes Araku Special

Highland Terroir

Grown in the highlands of Araku Valley, an internationally recognized region for premium specialty coffee with distinctive flavor profiles.

Direct Trade Model

We connect farmers directly with buyers and end consumers, preserving freshness and ensuring fair value at every stage of the supply chain.

Regenerative Farming

Shade-grown under regenerative farming systems that support biodiversity and maintain soil health for sustainable production.

100% Organic Certified

Fully certified organic from cultivation through processing, with complete traceability and third-party verification.

Tribal & Smallholder Farmers

Global Recognition
