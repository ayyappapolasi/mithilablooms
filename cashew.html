Superior Quality Cashews

Our cashews are premium export-grade kernels grown in the coastal belts of Visakhapatnam and Anakapalli districts, harvested by skilled local farmers and processed to strict international standards. These districts are known for producing cashews with superior size, creamy texture, and a naturally rich flavor, making them highly valued in global markets.

Export Grades We Produce

Five core categories tailored to buyer needs and international grading practices

W240 - Premium Large Kernel

Specification: ~240 kernels per pound

Size: Larger whole kernel, premium appearance

Best For: High-end retail, gift packs, gourmet snack tins, luxury confectionery

Highly preferred for visual appeal, whiteness, and premium positioning in export markets. Ideal for customers seeking the finest quality.

W320 - Standard High-Demand Grade

Specification: ~300-320 kernels per pound

Size: Medium-large whole kernel

Best For: Bulk retail packs, snack mixes, bakery products, confectionery

Clean appearance with consistent size and excellent value. Widely used for value-added food applications while maintaining superior quality.

Splits & Broken (S/BB)

Grade: Split and broken kernel pieces

Quality: Food-grade, carefully sorted

Best For: Industrial applications, ground products, food processing

Excellent for manufacturers and processors seeking cost-effective premium cashew material.

Raw Cashew Nuts (RCN)

Form: Unprocessed raw nuts in shell

Quality: Premium processing-grade

Best For: Nut processing facilities, commercial roasting

Ideal for buyers who operate their own processing and roasting facilities.
